What is the Pudgy Penguins Christmas distribution?
Pudgy Penguins Christmas is a seasonal token distribution. You can check eligibility, review your allocation, and claim during your window.
How do I check my eligibility and allocation?
Connect your Solana wallet and use the "Check Reward" / "Claim Reward" buttons. If eligible, the page will show your Pudgy Penguins Christmas allocation.
Which network and wallet do I need?
The Pudgy Penguins Christmas distribution runs on Solana. Use a trusted Solana wallet (for example, Phantom) and keep your seed phrase private.
